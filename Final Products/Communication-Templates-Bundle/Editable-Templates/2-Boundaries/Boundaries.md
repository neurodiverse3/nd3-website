# Boundaries

*Protecting your time, energy, or space going forward.*

## 9. Please don't call, text instead

*Use when: phone calls cost you a lot and you need contact to happen in writing.*

**Warm / Message**

> Quick ask: would you mind texting rather than calling? I take things in much better in writing, and I'll always reply properly. Nothing personal about calls, it's just how my brain works best. x
>

**Warm / Email**

Subject: **A small request on how we keep in touch**

Hi [name], I wanted to ask something that makes a real difference for me: could we keep things to text or email rather than phone calls where possible? I process written words far better and I'm much more reliable that way. I promise it's not about avoiding you; quite the opposite. Thank you. [you]

**Neutral / Message**

> Could you text rather than call? I take things in better in writing and will reply properly.
>

**Neutral / Email**

Subject: **Text rather than call, if you can**

Hi [name], where possible, could we use text or email instead of phone calls? I process written communication much more reliably. Thanks for understanding. [you]

**Brief / Message**

> Can you text rather than call? Easier for me to take in. Thanks x
>

**Brief / Email**

Subject: **Text not call, please**

Hi [name], please text or email rather than call where you can. Much easier for me. Thanks. [you]
